📜 Privacy Policy – Bseba App

Last Updated: February 2026

Bseba App provides a multi-service platform including doctor booking, house rental listings, blood bank requests, part-time jobs, home services, health checker tools, and user support features. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1️⃣ Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Full Name
  • Date of Birth / Age
  • Gender
  • Phone Number
  • District / Division / Area / Thana
  • Profile Photo (optional)

Health-Related Data

  • Blood Group
  • Appointment booking details
  • Health checker inputs (symptoms selection only)
  • Medical service requests

User-Generated Content

  • Blood requests
  • Rental posts
  • Job posts
  • Home service requests
  • Uploaded receipts
  • Chat messages

Financial / Reward Data

  • Submitted medical expense receipts
  • Reward points history

App Activity

  • Booking requests
  • Service interactions
  • Notifications
  • Ratings & reviews

2️⃣ How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Facilitate doctor appointments
  • Connect blood donors & recipients
  • Publish rental & job listings
  • Provide home service matching
  • Process reward points and cashback claims
  • Verify submitted receipts
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve app functionality
  • Send service notifications

Important: We do not use this data for medical diagnosis or treatment.

3️⃣ Data Sharing

We share your data only when necessary:

  • Between service requesters & providers (after acceptance)
  • Between blood donors & recipients
  • Between job posters & applicants
  • With admins for verification purposes

We do not sell your personal data.

4️⃣ Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and administrative safeguards to protect your data. No system can be 100% secure; by using this app, you acknowledge this risk.

5️⃣ Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary for:

  • Service functionality
  • Legal compliance
  • Dispute resolution

6️⃣ User Control

You may:

  • Update your profile
  • Delete posts or uploaded files
  • Request account deletion

7️⃣ Children’s Privacy

Bseba is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8️⃣ Contact

For questions or concerns, contact: support@bseba.com
